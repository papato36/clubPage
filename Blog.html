Our Certificates & Events for 2022-2023

The Department of Computer Engineering in association with TechForge CLUB conducted an event called “QUIZZLE”.


A tech related event that tested participants knowledge and gave them a platform to showcase their ideas as well! This competition was witnessed by 93 participants from various Engineering branches of NHCE.


QUIZZLE comprised of 2 rounds:
Round 1: Crossword
The students will be given crosswords on general knowledge and current affairs. Round 2: Quiz
The students will be given an array of questions about basic engineering sciences. Final evaluation of the participants was based on their highest score on the Round-2 Quiz. Dean Management Studies Dr Sheelan Mishra , The Head of the Computer Engineering department Dr SP Manikandan then revealed the two winning teams and presented them with their prizes.
